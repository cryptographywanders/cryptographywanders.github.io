Caeser Cipher

How to Solve

When solving the Caeser cipher, the player is given an encrypted message ,a cipher and a shift key.
To decrypt the message, the player will find the encrypted letter on the inner circle of the cipher to find
the decrypted letter on the outer circle. A shift key number is used to shift the positions of the
inner circles(both inner letter circle and number circle). If the shift key = right shift 3 ,
then shift the positions of the inner letter circle to right side 3 positions next,
and shift the positions of the inner number circle to left side 3 positions. If the shift key =left shift 3,
then shift the positions of the inner letter circle to left side 3 positions next, and shift the positions of the
inner number circle to right side 3 positions.
Note:
* Participants should find the both decrypted message
and the decrypted number values.

*The outer circle should be stable.

*Complete number of positions must be moved,if 3 then
three letters should be moved and start with 4th position.

Ex: Encrypted message = AYCQCP
Shift key : right shift 3
So,
Decrypted message : CAESER
Decrypted Number value: 6 4 8 22 8 21

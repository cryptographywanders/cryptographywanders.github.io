Multiplicative Cipher

How to Solve

Consider the letters and the associated numbers to be used as shown below,

To generate Multiplicative cipher, use the below formula:


(Alphabet Number * key)mod(total number of alphabets)

To encrypt the plain text and to find cipher text, key and plain text will given.
Use the above formula for each letter to be encrypted.
Ex:
Key = 4
Alphabet = S
-------------------------
(18*3)%26 = 2 , B --> 2
Note: Convert the decimals to whole numbers.


How to find mod?
Ex: 35%26 --> 35/26 =1.346154(round of to 1)
-->1*26=26
-->35-26=9(9 is marked to the letter to J, so J is the answer)
